How to publish my idea?

Set Value

You will set an ETH initial value for your idea. This value is your idea price whatever you set it. Etherdea users will be able to buy it with this price.

Publish

Fill the form for your idea. you will set a title for your idea and add your idea's content, images, files what ever you want to share it with others. Finally click on publish!

Get Paid!

Now everyone can see your idea and buy it if they like it!

How can I make sure that my Idea is protected?

Etherdea is blockchain based what means 

1

Property Ownership

You are the owner of your idea. No one can say that they are the owner because it's recorded in the blockchain and no one can tamper with the data.

2

Ideas as Digital Asset (Tokenization)

Your idea is your tokens, your money. You will be able to trade ideas.  

3

Check the Property Ownership

Etherdea will check if your idea is truly your by using an algorithm for that so no one has to verify that's your idea.
